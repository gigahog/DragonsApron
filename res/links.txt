Oubliette - confine the prisoners in a dark and narrow space and leave them in solitude for psychological torture. Sometimes, an additional form of torture was also used on political prisoners to extract information. Oubliette that had spikes coming up from the floor. The word Oubliette comes from the French word “Oublier” and literally means “to forget”.
